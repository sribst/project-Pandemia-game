PANDEMIA

Objectives:

You have 90 seconds to save the world !

Avoid the virus and get as many masks as you can to stay alive : one mask gets you 5 points.

Be careful : each time the virus gets you, you lose 10 points and your health deteriorates.

If your health goes down to 0, you are infected !

Once you collect enough masks to get to 100 points of health, the vaccine is unlocked !

Get to it before the timer ends and save the world !

GAME OVER

You are infected !

YOU WON

You just saved the world !
